[ PROGRAM DESCRIPTION ]

Offline Comedy operates an underground live comedy event network. Our SMS program is used to:

  • Notify subscribers of new show announcements in their local area
  • Facilitate ticket purchases via a conversational SMS flow
  • Deliver show detail updates (e.g., confirmed location) prior to event date

Messages are sent only to users who have explicitly opted in through offlinecomedy.com.

[ HOW TO OPT IN ]

To receive SMS messages from Offline Comedy, you must visit offlinecomedy.com, enter your mobile phone number and zip code, and check the consent checkbox confirming you agree to receive automated SMS messages. Opting in is entirely voluntary.

[ MESSAGE FREQUENCY ]

Message frequency varies. You will only receive messages when a new show is announced or scheduled in your area, or when you are actively participating in a ticket purchase conversation. Expect approximately 1–4 messages per show announcement.

Example messages you may receive:


"Offline announced. 4.15.26. Go offline? Reply Y to claim your spot or N to pass. Msg&data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe."


"How many will be going offline? Reply with the number of guests (e.g., 2)."


"Your offline tickets: [link]. Show details drop 24hrs before. Tell no one. Reply STOP to opt out."

Offline Comedy does not charge for SMS messages. However, standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Contact your carrier for details.

[ HOW TO OPT OUT ]

You may unsubscribe from Offline Comedy SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message we send. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. You may re-subscribe at any time by visiting offlinecomedy.com.

[ HOW TO GET HELP ]

Reply HELP to any Offline Comedy text message to receive support information. You may also contact us directly at hello@offlinecomedy.com.
